Generally about Computers
− An electronic device for storing and processing data, according to instructions given to it in a variable program

The computer or the multi-purpose computing device

Computers are powerful electronic devices that process information, perform calculations and solve problems through logical and mathematical operations. They have become an big part of our daily lives changing how we work, learn and interact with each others. The history of computers began in the early 20th century when mechanical devices like the tabulating machine were used for simple calculations. The kind of computing that surrounds us today began in the mid-20th century with the development of electronic computers like the ENIAC, which marked a significant change in computer development. Over time computers evolved from machines filling hole rooms to compact devices like laptops and smartphones. Computers still continue to advance to this day, shaping the way we communicate, work and connect with others.

An introduction to the inner workings of a computer

In a computer system, components work together to complete a series of tasks to make the computer function as intended. At the center lays the CPU, executing instructions and calculations equations. The motherboard serves as a hub for connecting the CPU, RAM, storage drives, and other components. The RAM acts as the systems short term memory, storing data that the CPU needs for quick access. Storage devices such as HDDs, or SSDs, retain data permanently. The GPU renders images and videos, for your computer screen. Networking components facilitate communication between the computer, the internet and other devices. Together the components create a cohesive system, enabling the computer to perform a wide range of functions. From easy calculations to complex simulations, which increases productivity and decrases our time spent on repetitive tasks.
